Part 2 · the most important section

Pick your audience first.

This is Bailey's whole framework. A profitable Etsy audience has 3 traits:

A profitable audience has all 3
  1. Continuous need for new products. Not one-and-done buyers.
  2. Their buying changes with seasons, holidays, and trends. Constant fresh demand.
  3. You can make 3 to 5+ different product types for them. Cohesive shop, repeat customers.
Examples Bailey gives: sublimation crafters, SVG/Cricut crafters, junk journal makers, teachers, nurses, brides, dog moms, faith-based women, readers, gardeners, fishing dads, sports parents, small business owners. Or pick from your own life.
If you'd get bored in 30 days, skip it. You'll be making a lot of designs. Pick something you won't hate.
An IG account, TikTok, Pinterest, email list, a community you're in, family/friends in the niche, a YouTube channel, a Facebook group? Or are you starting from zero?
