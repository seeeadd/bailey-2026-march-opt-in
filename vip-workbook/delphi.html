How to Use Delphi in 5 Steps

1
Finish your Workbook (Day 3)
Complete all 3 days of the VIP Workbook. At the end of Day 3, click Generate My Report. The more detailed your answers, the better your personalized plan will be.
2
Download your Track Report (PDF)
After your report generates, click the gold Download Your Track Report button at the bottom. It saves as a PDF to your phone or computer. You can also screenshot it if PDF doesn't work.
3
Open Bailey Delphi
Check your email for the Delphi invite (arrives within 24 hours of VIP purchase). Click the link to open it. It works on your phone, tablet, or computer — no app needed.
4
Upload your Report + paste the prompt
Inside Delphi, upload your Track Report PDF (or paste the text). Then go back to your Workbook, find the Copy This Prompt button at the bottom of your report, and paste the prompt into Delphi right after your report. Hit send.
5
Read your personalized plan
Delphi reads your entire situation — your life, kids, schedule, niche, fears, goals — and gives you a coaching session like you're sitting across from Bailey. Everyone uses the same prompt, but because YOUR answers are different, you get a completely different plan.

Common Questions

What if I haven't finished my Workbook yet? ▼
You can still open Delphi and ask it questions anytime! But the personalized coaching session works best when your Track Report is complete. The report is what gives Delphi all the context about your life. Without it, you'll get good advice but it won't be tailored to you.
Can I use this on my phone? ▼
Yes! Delphi works in any browser on any device. No app to download. Just open the link from your email, upload your report, and paste the prompt. Works great on iPhone, Android, iPad, or computer.
I can't find my Delphi invite email. Help! ▼
Check your Promotions tab (Gmail) or spam/junk folder. The invite comes from Bailey Vann within 24 hours of your VIP purchase. Search for "Delphi" or "Bailey Vann" in your email. Still can't find it? Email hello@baileyvann.com and we'll resend it.
What if Delphi gives me a weird or confusing answer? ▼
Just ask it to clarify! Type something like "Can you explain that in simpler terms?" or "Give me the step-by-step for a complete beginner." Delphi is patient. You can ask follow-up questions all day. It won't judge you.
Can I come back and use Delphi again later? ▼
Absolutely. Delphi is included with your VIP access. Use it as much as you want. Come back in a week when you're stuck, or in a month when you want to plan your next step. It's like having Bailey on speed dial.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT? ▼
Delphi is trained specifically on everything Bailey has taught — her courses, YouTube videos, Q&As, coaching calls, strategies. ChatGPT gives generic advice. Delphi gives you Bailey's actual advice, frameworks, and methods. It knows her system inside and out.
